The problem

It never fails when you're watching

Gear doesn't announce when it lapses, and a spreadsheet won't warn you. The failure surfaces at the worst possible moment — on the range, in an emergency, or when you file a claim.

An expired tourniquet

Medications, hemostatic gauze and trauma kit pass their shelf life unnoticed.

A dead battery

The CR123 in the light, or the detector you only check when it's already too late.

An out-of-spec spring

5,000 rounds on a recoil spring with no count — reliability you can't see.

A lapsed permit

A carry permit, hunting licence or passport that quietly expired last month.

Ammo running out

No idea you're four weeks from empty at your current training cadence.

No proof for your insurer

Something is lost or stolen and you have no serials, photos or valuations on hand.

Private by architecture

Your data never leaves your control

Privacy isn't a setting in OpState — it's the architecture. There's no company server holding a database of who owns what, because there's no company server at all.

  • On-device, AES-256. Encrypted locally; keys live in the iOS Keychain / Android Keystore and unlock with biometrics.
  • Your cloud, not ours. Backup goes to your own iCloud or Google Drive. We never receive it.
  • No central database. No accounts to breach, no telemetry, no tracking. Crash reporting, if ever added, is opt-in only.
  • Swiss-built, GDPR & FADP minded. Metric and imperial, multilingual, with user-configurable per-country profiles.

Even this page practises it: no cookies, no analytics, no third-party fonts or scripts. The only thing that ever leaves your browser is your email — and only if you choose to join.

Good to know

When does it launch?

Soon — the apps run on both platforms today; what's left before release is notifications and a richer export. Join the waitlist for the date the moment it's set, and opt into weekly updates to follow along.

What will it cost?

OpState launches free and worldwide with everything unlocked. A Pro tier comes later — but join during early access and you keep Pro free for life. No card, no commitment to join the waitlist.

Is my data really private?

Yes — by design. Your inventory is AES-256 encrypted on your device, keys held in the iOS Keychain / Android Keystore, and any backup goes to your own iCloud or Google Drive. There's no central server, no account, and no tracking. We literally can't see your data.

iOS and Android?

Both, built together from one shared codebase so they look and behave identically. No "Android version six months later."

Is this a "gun app"?

It's a readiness and inventory app for anyone with gear that matters — medical, power and comms, documents, valuables — and yes, firearms and ammo if you have them. No purchase features, no legal advice; just tracking and reminders you configure yourself.
