Privacy

Waitlist privacy notice

Last updated: 15 June 2026

This notice covers only this website and its waitlist. The OpState app itself stores your data encrypted on your own device and backs up to your own cloud — the app sends us nothing. (More on that on the home page.)

What we collect

If — and only if — you submit the waitlist form, we collect:

  • Your email address.
  • Your weekly-updates preference (whether you opted into development updates).
  • Basic technical metadata our email provider records with any signup (e.g. timestamp, and the IP address used to confirm consent).

We do not use cookies, analytics, advertising trackers, third-party fonts, or any other scripts on this site. If you never submit the form, we receive nothing.

Why we collect it & the legal basis

We use your email to notify you when OpState launches and, if you opted in, to send a short weekly build update. The legal basis is your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a) / the Swiss FADP). Kit uses double opt-in, so your subscription is only active after you click the confirmation link we email you.

Who processes it

Email collection and delivery are handled by Kit (ConvertKit), acting as our processor. Your email is stored on their systems for this purpose only. See Kit's own privacy policy at kit.com/privacy. We do not sell, rent, or share your address with anyone else.

How long we keep it

Until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it, or until the waitlist purpose ends (we'll clear the list within a reasonable period after launch if you've unsubscribed). Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe.

Your rights

You can access, correct, export, or delete your data, and withdraw consent at any time — unsubscribe from any email, or contact us below and we'll action it. You may also lodge a complaint with your data-protection authority.

Contact

Email hello@opstate.app for any privacy request.

This is a plain-language notice for an early-access waitlist, not legal advice. It will be replaced by a full privacy policy at launch.
